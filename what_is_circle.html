What is Project Circle?

"Democratically weed out your boring friends through a minigame to determine who is the most popular player."

Project Circle is a game inspired by Jackbox, Amongs Us and various other titles. One very important thing they have in common is the fact that they are free/low price minigames for you and your friends to play in. During these hard times, in which most of us find ourselves unnable to connect often with friends in person, we created this concept to continue adding more outlets for people to use when they most need to reconnect with friends.

The creative and narrative aspects of the game are precisely what make it different from its counterparts. It unifies the experience of playing different minigames with a larger strategic game that encourages building relationships with other players. Finally, those relationships are tested by forcing players to rank their alliances, voting each other out until one player is victorious.
